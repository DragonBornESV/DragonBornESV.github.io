What I do

I enjoy building interactive experiences across game development, XR, and hardware-based systems. What interests me most is taking an idea from concept or sketch to something tangible, testable, and usable — whether that is a game system, an AR application, or a physical prototype.

🎮 Game Development

Designing and building interactive experiences focused on mechanics, systems, player engagement, and gameplay feel — mainly in Unity and C#.

🥽 AR / VR Applications

Building AR and VR applications for learning, visualization, experimentation, and immersive interaction, primarily with Unity and AR Foundation.

🔧 Hardware & Prototyping

Creating Arduino-based systems, controllers, and physical prototypes that connect software with sensors, motors, LEDs, and real-world interaction.

🎨 3D & Fabrication

Practical 3D modeling and 3D printing for prototypes, mechanical parts, and interactive projects — mainly in Fusion 360, with Blender when needed.
